Welcome to the World of Minions!

The Minions are small, yellow, cylindrical creatures who speak in a unique language but are often understood to say "Bello" by humans.

Kevin

Kevin is the leader of the Minions. He is known for his strategic planning skills and leadership qualities.

Stuart

Stuart is the smallest of the three main minions. He is clumsy and often causes accidents but is very loyal.

Bob

Bob is the youngest of the trio. He is naive and innocent but has a big heart and is very brave.

Otto

Otto is a new minion introduced in Minions: The Rise of Gru. He is adventurous and loves to explore.
